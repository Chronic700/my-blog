## From Idea to Reality

Every revolutionary product, service, or movement started as a "crazy" idea in someone's mind. The difference between dreamers and builders is execution.

### Steps to Execute Big Ideas

1. **Crystallize the vision** - Make it concrete
2. **Break it down** - Chunk it into manageable pieces
3. **Build momentum** - Start with small wins
4. **Iterate relentlessly** - Improve based on feedback
5. **Scale intelligently** - Grow sustainably
